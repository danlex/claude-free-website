## What they do
The Free Web is an open-source starter kit by TVL Technology that lets anyone — including complete non-developers — build and publish a professional website for free using Claude AI and GitHub Pages.

TVL Technology is a full-service software agency specializing in AI-driven innovation, blockchain infrastructure, and custom web development.

## Target audience
- Small business owners who need a website but can't afford an agency
- Freelancers and consultants who want a professional online presence
- Individuals building personal brand or portfolio sites
- Early-stage startups who need to move fast
- Non-technical founders

## Key differentiators
- Completely free (GitHub Pages hosting, open-source repo)
- AI-guided: no commands to memorize, just answer questions
- Includes a researcher that builds your knowledge base from URLs + documents
- Generates real content from your background — no Lorem Ipsum
- Ships in ~15 minutes from zero to live

## How it works (5 steps)
1. Install Claude Code and clone the repo
2. Start Claude — it auto-greets you as a Website Architect
3. Optionally share your LinkedIn/website — Claude researches your background
4. Claude builds your site and shows a live preview
5. Claude pushes to GitHub and enables GitHub Pages — your site is live
