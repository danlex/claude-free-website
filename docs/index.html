How It Works

FIVE STEPS FROM
ZERO TO LIVE

You answer questions. Claude does everything else.

  1. 01

    Install Claude Code & clone the repo

    Download Claude Code (free), then clone the open-source Free Web repository to your machine. No other tools, runtimes, or accounts required.

  2. 02

    Start Claude — it greets you as your Website Architect

    Open the project in Claude Code. The AI automatically introduces itself and walks you through a short guided conversation to understand what your site needs to accomplish.

  3. 03

    Share your LinkedIn or website — Claude researches your background

    Optionally paste a URL or upload documents. The AI Researcher reads your background and builds a knowledge base so your site has real, accurate content — not Lorem Ipsum.

  4. 04

    Claude builds your site and shows a live preview

    The AI Builder generates a complete, professional HTML/CSS/JS site. A local preview opens in your browser so you can review exactly what visitors will see before publishing.

  5. 05

    Claude pushes to GitHub and enables Pages — you’re live

    One command. Claude creates your GitHub repository, pushes the site, and enables GitHub Pages. Your site is live at a public URL — for free, forever.
